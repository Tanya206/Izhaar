About Us

At Izhaar, we're driven by a profound mission to break down communication barriers for the Deaf and Hard of Hearing community. We are passionate about creating a more inclusive world through technology. We've developed cutting-edge sign language detection software facilitating real-time communication and promoting accessibility. Our vision is clear: to empower the Deaf and Hard of Hearing community and ensure that sign language is understood and respected as a vital form of communication. Join us on this inspiring journey towards a more connected world.
